NetDAG Roadmap

From presale to mainstream utility — measured milestones that prioritize stability, transparency, and real-world use.

We publish clear phases so the community can verify progress. Dates reflect target windows and may shift to prioritize security, audits, and partner readiness. Major changes are announced on the site.

Legend: ● delivered/complete · ● planned/target

Phase 0 — Foundations (Q3–Q4 2025)

  • Whitepaper, site architecture, branding, and i18n scaffolding
  • Tokenomics finalization: 1B max, presale at $0.03, reserve & vesting parameters
  • Smart contract specs for bonding curve & reserve safety
  • Guardian design doc: data pipeline, anomaly classes, governance flow
  • Presale infrastructure & audit kickoff

Phase 1 — Presale & Public Curve Activation (Nov–Dec 2025)

  • Ambassador program launch and community education
  • Presale opens (target 30 Nov 2025)
  • Public curve activation (site countdown targets 14 Dec 2025, 00:00 CET)
  • Reserve locking (22% permanent shock-absorber backing)
  • Initial partner pilots for Provenance

Phase 2 — Guardian Beta & Provenance Pilots (H1 2026)

  • Guardian beta (read-only proposals; DAO/multisig approval required)
  • Public dashboards: liquidity, curve state, anomaly insights
  • Provenance pilot launches (food, fashion, pharma)
  • dVPN closed alpha (node registration, staking, basic routing)
  • Security audits — iterative hardening

Phase 3 — Utility Expansion & dVPN Apps (H2 2026–H1 2027)

  • Provenance self-serve portal for brands and 3rd-party integrators
  • dVPN mobile + desktop apps (pay-as-you-go via NDG)
  • Guardian routing & quality scoring for dVPN nodes
  • Treasury & ecosystem grants (education, dev tooling)

Phase 4 — Governance Maturation (2027–2028)

  • DAO phase-in for curve parameters & treasury proposals
  • Guardian v2 (predictive stress-testing; structured policy bundles)
  • Deeper exchange & wallet integrations
  • Regulatory readiness and disclosures per jurisdiction

Phase 5 — Global Adoption & Enterprise Integrations (2028–2030+)

  • Enterprise APIs for Provenance and payments settlements
  • Commerce plugins (POS, e-commerce, logistics platforms)
  • Guardian v3 (multi-market signals; broader anomaly models)
  • Cross-ecosystem partnerships and L2/rollup bridges if beneficial

Throughout all phases: 2% charity flow, transparency reports, continuous audits.

Key Near-Term Milestones

  • Presale launch & public curve activation
  • Guardian beta (advisory mode) with dashboards
  • Provenance pilots in at least 2 verticals
  • dVPN alpha (node staking & routing)
  • Audit reports and live transparency pages
  • Ambassador growth across key regions
