Inside NetDAG Guardian: Proactive Liquidity & Risk Signals

Published · Oct 8, 2025

The NetDAG Guardian is our AI layer that watches the network’s pulse and acts before stress shows up on-chain.

By ingesting order-flow, volatility regimes, reserve levels, and cross-market signals, the Guardian produces short-horizon forecasts of liquidity needs and price pressure. Those signals inform automated guardrails and human-reviewed parameter changes.

What it does: forecasts demand, detects anomalies, proposes parameter tweaks (fees, curve slopes, reserve taps) and escalates when outliers appear.

Stability isn’t about freezing markets; it’s about shaping trajectories. With Guardian guidance, NetDAG’s bonding-curve and reserve mechanisms stay responsive without becoming reactive.
