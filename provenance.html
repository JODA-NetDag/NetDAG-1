    Trust Written in Code: NetDAG Provenance

    Authenticity as on-chain proof — scannable by anyone, instantly.

    What is NetDAG Provenance?

    NetDAG Provenance is a proof-of-origin system. When a buyer scans a product’s QR tag, they see immutable data from the blockchain — where it was made, when it shipped, and who certified it — not a marketing page.

    How It Works: The Journey to Verifiable Trust

    1) Registration

    Verified producers upload batch details and documents to the Provenance Portal.

    2) Blockchain Record

    A smart contract secures the record (hash + metadata). Certifiers co-sign where required.

    3) Instant Verification

    Consumers scan the product’s QR to reveal batch data, certifiers, timestamps — and a tamper-evident audit trail.

    4) Value Loop

    Each scan triggers a tiny NDG tip back to the ecosystem — rewarding honest producers and network validators.
